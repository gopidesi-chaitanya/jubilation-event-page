Code Conflux

Code Conflux offers participants the opportunity to showcase their skills in both knowledge and coding abilities. The online quiz contest in level one tests their understanding of concepts, while the coding round in level two challenges them to solve problems across various difficulty levels. This format allows participants to demonstrate their expertise, learn from the experience, and potentially win prizes or recognition for their achievements in the coding community
